# [HFCF-SPCs TPAMI 2026](https://kaustubh-sadekar.github.io/High-Flux-Count-Free-Single-Photon-3D-Cameras/)
A hardware-algorithm co-design to jointly address the two major bottlenecks of single-photon 3D cameras: (i) extreme data rates, (ii) dead-time-distorted measurements in high-flux scenarios. Our proposed high-flux count-free single-photon 3D cameras (HFCF-SPCs) can recover accurate scene distances from highly distorted, low-memory measurements. This work pushes SPCs one step closer to mainstream 3D cameras for resource-constrained dense 3D sensing applications operating under a wide range of illumination conditions.
